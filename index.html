Exhibit AValuation

Equity Valuation: Novo Nordisk — with a Two-Years-On Retrospective

A full valuation of Novo Nordisk built during my internship at Prima Ekuiti: three-statement model, discounted cash flow and comparable company analysis, concluding in a BUY recommendation with 10.2% upside, presented to a panel of eight portfolio managers.

Alongside the original model sits a short retrospective written in 2026: what the thesis was, what actually happened to the business and the share price since, what the model got right and wrong, and what I would change today. Revisiting a valuation with hindsight is, I think, the fastest way to learn what drives one.

Exhibit DQuantitative

Quantitative Research in R — Two Projects

Backtesting & parameter optimisation. A trading-strategy engine built on a 15-year, 300MB+ Compustat daily dataset: custom signal-generation and backtesting functions, an optimisation loop across 16 parameter combinations, and an honest conclusion — both strategies underperformed the benchmark, and the write-up explains the mechanism rather than the hoped-for outcome.

Factor regression modelling (group of 4). A cross-sectional analysis of 502 S&P 500 tickers, culminating in a Fama–French three-factor regression: interpreting betas, significance, and an R² of 0.05 showing that over 94% of the security’s return variation was idiosyncratic rather than factor-driven.
